OUR STORY

Who We Are

Atari Farms is a Ugandan climate-smart agribusiness focused on reducing post-harvest losses, improving market access for small holder farmers and transforming agricultural produce into high-value products through cicualr innovation.

Founded in 2019, Atari Farms works with farmers, youth and women across Eastern Uganda to build resilent and inclusive agricultural value chains through aggregation, processing, market linkage and value addition.

The Challenge We Address

Smallholder farmers face several barriers that limit productivity and income growth. Atari Farms was established to build market-driven and circular agricultural systems.

Market Access

Limited access to reliable and organised markets.

Post-harvest Losses

High losses due to poor handling and storage systems.

Low Value Addition

Most produce is sold without processing or branding.

Youth Opprortunities

Limited participation of young people in agribusiness.
